Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AKSA 10X FASTER add-ins.
What is AKSA 10X FASTER?
AKSA 10X FASTER is a professional ribbon add-in for Microsoft Word (and soon Excel, Photoshop, Illustrator) that adds powerful productivity tools — legal templates, AI writing, number conversion, date tools, and more — directly inside your application.
Is it really free for 30 days?
Yes. You get full access to all features for 30 days with no credit card required. After the trial, you can purchase a license to continue using premium features (JPG save, Math tools, AI features).
Which versions of Word are supported?
Microsoft Word 2016 and later (64-bit version). The add-in is built as a COM add-in and works with Office 365, Office 2019, Office 2021, and older 2016 versions.
How do I get a license key?
You can request a free trial key from our website. For paid licenses, make a payment via bKash/Nagad/Rocket to 01670201266 and submit your transaction ID on the purchase page. You will receive your key within 24 hours.
Does one license work for all add-ins?
Yes. One license key works across all AKSA 10X FASTER products — Word, Excel, Photoshop, and Illustrator. You don't need separate keys for each product.
Can I use it on multiple computers?
A single license is intended for use on one computer. If you need to use it on multiple machines, please purchase separate licenses for each installation.
